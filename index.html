The centre is probably best known for its wave pool. When the waves are off, the pool is also used for leisure swims and other aquatic activities. Check out our public swimming schedule. For more information, visit us, or call 613-591-WAVE (9283).

The Kanata Leisure Centre is conveniently located at 70 Aird Place adjacent to Hwy #417 at the corner of Castlefrank and Aird Place in the community of Kanata. Travelling westbound on Hwy 417, take exit 139 (Castlefrank Road) and proceed south over the Queensway (Hwy 417). Turn left onto Aird Place. Travelling eastbound on Hwy 417, take exit 140 (Terry Fox Drive). Turn right onto Terry Fox, left onto Katimavik, left onto Castlefrank, and right onto Aird Place.

Wave pools provide the perfect destination for visitors to our city including bus tours and group outings. Residents can also enjoy the "surf" or take a leisurely swim when the waves are off. There is also a kiddie pool with spray fountains and whirlpool. The facility also boasts a brand new 25m lap pool with an accessible ramp and a new 32m waterslide!
